Engineering Strategies & Practice II (2022)

Our team was tasked by our client to come up with a solution or preventative measure for grime accumulating on their apartment windows. To create the best possible solution, we researched the causes and the current solutions on the market, held 3 meetings with our client to ask questions, and applied the engineering design process to funnel our ideas. Our top design was a titanium dioxide coating with which we created a testable prototype in a lab setting.

Engineering Strategies & Practice I (2021)

Worked as the leader of my first year design team. Our task was to create an add on to a delivery robot to make it more accessibility friendly. I helped keep my team organized by implementing a weekly planner and designating tasks. Administered the engineering design process to funnel our ideas down to 3 alternative designs that cover most of the design space. As a team, we created a culmulative document describing our process of how we reached our top 3 solutions.

Global Brigades (August 2022)

Participated in the University of Toronto Engineering Brigade as a student volunteer. Raised money and travelled to Honduras for 1 week to help the community of Parihuaca, Nacaome by designing a water system. The community had lacked a safe and convenient source of water as both their well and dam became inoperative. Volunteer work consisted of communicating with townsfolk, taking measurements for the pipeline, mapping out the system and conducting a presentation to the community to showcase the design and budget.

i4 Competition (2021)

Competed in a group of 4 in the University of Waterloo run i4 Competition. It was a one day manufacturing engineering based competition that tested our time-management and analytical skills. We were given a simulation of a production line with which we analyzed the manufacturing process and noted the process, layout, and efficiency of the overall system. From this, we wrote status reports of our progress, completed each milestone task and eventually came up with a way to optimize the production line.
